ABOUT

A genius Onmyouji Master that inherited the ancestral treasure Seimei Umbrella. As descendant of Onmyouji Master Seimei, Kagura had been an unruly, quirky child ever since she lost her parents at an early age. But for some mysterious reason, the treasure Seimei Umbrella chose her to be its rightful owner. Kagura is a dance based on Japanese mythology, translated as "entertaining the gods." Originally called "kamukura" or "kamikura," kagura dances are thought to have been very simple, similar to miko (shrine maiden) dances or puppet dances. Kagura is performed in many other parts of Japan.
